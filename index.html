Our Story

Welcome to the London Academy of Bhangra, your new prestigious dance academy in East London dedicated to promoting and preserving the rich heritage of Bhangra and Punjabi culture.

Whether you're a complete beginner or an experienced dancer, our classes are designed for all abilities. Join us to learn, connect and celebrate the rhythm, history and joy of Bhangra.
