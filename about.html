The Co-Founders

Ravi (left) is a highly skilled and well-trained Bhangra dancer, co-founder of the London Academy of Bhangra, and a prominent figure in the UK Bhangra scene. Ravi's Bhangra journey began in 2016, and since then he has competed in 13 competitions, showcasing his verstility, experience and passion for both musical and live forms of Bhangra.

Gurdeep (right) is one of the UK's most decorated Bhangra dancers and co-founder of the London Academy of Bhangra. Dancing since the age of 11, Gurdeep's unmatched passion for Punjabi dance and music has driven him to become a celebrated figure in the global bhangra scene. Trained under some of the UK's most senior Bhangra figures, Gurdeep has honed his craft to become one of the best dancers in the country.
